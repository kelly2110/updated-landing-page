Plan your Curriculum with the MSPCourseBuilder

Developed by MSP students for MSP students, MSPCourseBuilder is the ultimate tool to plan out your schedule for your time at Maastricht Science Programme.

Get started

Why use MSPCourseBuilder?

The goal of MSPCourseBuilder is to aid students in the course-selection process and to provide an easy-accessible and clear overview of all the possibilities for their choice of courses. Our Curriculum Planner allows you to guide yourself through your journey of MSP in just a few clicks. With our tool, you can plan ahead just your upcoming period or even your whole curriculum.

Choose Courses for your Upcoming Semester

Struggling to pick your courses for next semester? Or not sure if your preferred combination of courses is possible? MSPCourseBuilder is here to help! Quickly see what your options are for the upcoming periods by taking a look at the Curriculum Planner.

02

Plan your Journey at MSP

Want to know what your options are for your entire MSP journey? Our Curriculum Planner gives you the possibility to plan out your whole career at MSP, and will even tell you whether you meet all the necessary graduation requirements with your selected courses!

Find Information in the Course Wiki

Looking for specific information about a course or just want to know more? Take a look at our Course Wiki! In here, you can find all the information you need, from pre/co-requisites and assessment to course-coordinator contact information and BTR information.
